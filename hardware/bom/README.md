# Bills of materials

Store one reviewed bill of materials per hardware revision or historical
delivery.

The archived 2024 workbook is at [`2024/bom.xlsx`](2024/bom.xlsx). Before
reuse, check:

- quantity per kit and total quantity;
- manufacturer part number;
- electrical and mechanical specifications;
- acceptable substitutions;
- supplier links, stock and current pricing;
- minimum order quantities;
- consumables and spares; and
- the PCB revision to which the BOM applies.

Avoid making a single supplier's stock code the only component identifier.
