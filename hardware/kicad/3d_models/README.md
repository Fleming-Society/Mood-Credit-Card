# 3D model placeholders

The KiCad boards reference models for the ESP32-C3 SuperMini and MAX30102
module, but the supplied STEP files are not distributed here because their
copyright and redistribution terms have not been confirmed.

Local copies are held in the ignored `private/licensing-review/3d-models/`
directory while their provenance is investigated. Replace them with models
whose licences permit redistribution, then record their authors, sources and
licences in the root [`NOTICE.md`](../../../NOTICE.md).

The PCB and schematic sources remain usable without the 3D models. KiCad's 3D
viewer will show the corresponding components without models until suitable
replacements are added.
